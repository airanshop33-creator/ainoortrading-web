About Ainoor Trading

Our journey began with a single vision: to showcase the hidden wealth of Indonesia's seas through the finest seaweed. Ainoor Trading was founded on the shores of local coastal communities, establishing a direct bridge from well-organized networks of traditional fishermen to the global market. Every single strand of our seaweed is meticulously hand-harvested, sun-dried, and chosen with care by coastal families who have mastered this craft for generations.

By accommodating and empowering these local fishermen, we don't just secure premium-grade raw materials; we also build an ethical, sustainable supply chain. We manage the entire process—serving as a trusted international marketing powerhouse and maintaining a strict, multi-tier Quality Control (QC) system right at the source. Partnering with us means securing consistency and premium quality, while directly fostering sustainable livelihoods and marine conservation in Indonesia. Let’s grow a sustainable future together!

Our Premium Products

Eucheuma Cottonii (Food Grade)

Premium sun-dried seaweed, meticulously cleaned for food manufacturing. Ideal as a high-yield raw material for carrageenan extraction, organic texturizers, and healthy seaweed snack products like premium nori sheets.

Liquid Organic Fertilizer

Eucheuma Cottonii Based

An eco-friendly bio-stimulant formulated entirely from nutrient-rich seaweed extract. It boosts crop resilience, enhances soil health, and accelerates root growth, making it a sustainable choice for modern organic farming.
