Liquid Organic Fertilizer (POC)

Eucheuma Cottonii Based

Our Liquid Organic Fertilizer (Pupuk Organik Cair) is an eco-friendly bio-stimulant formulated entirely from nutrient-rich marine seaweed extracts. It is engineered to naturally boost crop resilience against environmental stress, enhance internal soil biology, and accelerate root and vegetative growth. Formulated seamlessly for modern organic farming practices, it ensures sustainable agriculture without chemical toxicity.

Technical Specifications
Base Material 100% Pure Eucheuma cottonii Seaweed Extract
Form / Color Liquid concentrated solution, deep brown/green hue
Nutrient Profile Rich in Natural Auxins, Cytokinins, Gibberellins, and Trace Elements
Solubility 99.5% Water Soluble (Ideal for foliar spray & drip irrigation)
pH Level 6.0 - 7.5 (Balanced for optimal plant absorption)
Packaging Options Available in bulk jerrycans, drums, or custom export bottling
